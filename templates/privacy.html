Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. {{ site_name }} is a free, independent public-service tool. This page explains what we collect and why.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for or store your name.
  • We do not require an account, login, or any contact details.
  • We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

What we do store

To understand who we serve and improve the service, we store the answers you give in the eligibility checker — the state, age, gender, caste category, income range, occupation, land ownership, and the schemes you matched — under a random record ID, with no name attached.

We also record basic technical visit information so we can measure usage and keep the service secure: your IP address, browser/device type, and which stage of the check you reached (for example, whether you only visited or completed a check). This is standard server log data used for analytics and to prevent abuse.

How it is used

  • Aggregate analysis (e.g. how many people in each state check eligibility, which schemes are most relevant).
  • Understanding how visitors use the site, so we can improve it.
  • Keeping the service secure and preventing misuse.
  • Improving coverage and accuracy of the scheme database.

Your rights

We do not use your data to build a profile of you or contact you. If you have any question about this policy, or would like us to remove records associated with your IP address, please contact the site operator.
