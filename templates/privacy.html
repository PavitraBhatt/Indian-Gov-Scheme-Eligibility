Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. {{ site_name }} is a free, independent public-service tool. This page explains what we collect and why.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not ask for or store your name.
  • We do not store your IP address.
  • We do not require an account, login, or any contact details.
  • We never sell data or use it for advertising.

What we do store

To understand who we serve and improve the service, we store the answers you give in the eligibility checker — the state, age, gender, caste category, income range, occupation, land ownership, and the schemes you matched — as an anonymous record under a random ID. Because there is no name or IP address, these records are not linked to you personally.

How it is used

  • Aggregate analysis (e.g. how many people in each state check eligibility, which schemes are most relevant).
  • Improving coverage and accuracy of the scheme database.

Your rights

Because records are anonymous and hold no identifier, we cannot single out an individual's data. If you have any question about this policy, please contact the site operator.
